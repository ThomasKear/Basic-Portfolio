About Me>

Spent my younger years on Long Island, New York. Then my parents retired to the mountains of Hendersonville, NC. At first, I hated the region and couldn't wait to get out. Ran away twice, but mountains don't have road signs and just got lost twice.


Right after high school, joined the Marines, loved it at first, but soon relized it wasn't a long term relationship. Bartended all over DC and had a great time. Alas my liver told me, that too was not to be a long term relationship.


Fast forward upteen years and then I had my daughter, Beverly Ann. She made everything make sense. This, this is my long term relationship, everything else is just a means to an end. The only thing I can do to be the best father I know, is to always keep improving myself.
